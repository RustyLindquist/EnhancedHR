1. **Create a Collection** (“Onboarding” or “Product Enablement”)
2. **Add 3–5 inputs** (policy PDF, a doc, a slide, a video)
3. **Ask 3 questions** that prove grounding + usefulness
4. **Show scope** (“only answers from what’s inside”)
5. **Assign to a Group** (Engineering onboarding cohort)
6. **Show Measurement** (required learning + analytics assistant)
7. Optional: **RolePlay Dojo** as a “leader workflow tool” (fast wow)
